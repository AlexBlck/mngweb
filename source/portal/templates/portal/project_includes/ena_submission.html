Project title & description

The MicrobesNG project is committed to open science and a key component of our grant funding is that we will submit data to the European Nucleotide Archive following a 12-month embargo. This forms part of our terms for customers taking advantage of our discounted rates, but it does not apply to our full price confidential or industry rates.

We won't submit your data to the ENA until at least 12 months following data delivery, and we will forewarn you of submission closer to the time. We do require a project title and description before we can proceed with your project. This will form part of the ENA submission, and you will be able to update this at any time prior to publication.

Key points

  • For projects paid for at BBSRC or non-commercial discounted rates, your data will be eligible for automatic submission to the ENA 12 months after data delivery.
  • You will be reminded by email at 30 days prior to submission and asked to review your metadata.
  • You will have the opportunity to upgrade to a confidential status (at additional cost) prior to submission.
  • Confidential or industry projects will not be submitted unless requested.
  • We do require that all requested metadata is completed in full.
